Windy Day by the Ocean

I attempted to recreate the sounds that occur on a windy day by the ocean, with wind, waves, and a light piano tune in the background. I found the music, which is titled Headwaters(Instrumental) by Chad Crouch at a free music site Free Music archive. The sounds of wind and waves were (attempts) made using my own voice recordings and edited in the audio-editing app Audacity. The video of the ocean was found on the free video site Pexels. Using Shotcut as video-editing software, I pieced together all of the files into one video shown above.
